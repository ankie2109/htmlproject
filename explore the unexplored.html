  1. Mahendragiri

    Mahendragiri, is a mountain in Rayagada subdivision of the district of Gajapati, Odisha, India. It is the second highest peak of odisha, situated amongst the Eastern Ghats at an elevation of 1,501 metres (4,925 ft).Mahendragiri hill and its surrounding areas are recognized as a biodiversity hot spot due to numerous medicinal plants and other species that are found here. A haven for medicinal plants, Mahendragiri Hills, which is part of the Eastern Ghats, is home to over 600 flowering plants. The faunal diversity of the region is huge and particularly known for being a herpetofaunal hotspot.

  2. Deomali

    Deomali, is a mountain peak in the Chandragiri-Pottangi subrange of the Eastern Ghats. It is located near Koraput town in the Koraput district of southern Odisha, India.
    Deomali Peak, with an elevation of about 1,672 m, is the highest peak in the state of Odisha. It is situated near Doodhari village, Pottangi Panchayat division which is 70 km from Koraput via semiliguda, in Southern Odisha.

  3. Mankadadian Waterfall

    A marvellous place to visit and is located near R.Udaygiri, of gajapati district in odisha state,a pristine natural beauty near Kankada village under Sialilati panchayat of this block in Gajapati district.
    The waterfall drains down a hill from a 100ft height, creating a scenic beauty, which the tourists are bound to appreciate. The picturesque site is a tourists’ delight as one has to walk around 2km hilly tracks along a stream to reach there.

  4. Rani Duduma Waterfall

    Rani Duduma is a beautiful fall in Koraput District of Odisha. It is around 40-45 km from Koraput Town and 20 km from Semiliguda location. It is 7 km away from the village called Nandapur.This horsetail type waterfall is 175 metres (574 ft) in height and is formed by the Machkund river.
